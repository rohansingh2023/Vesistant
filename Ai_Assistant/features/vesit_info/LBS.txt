
LBS Championship 2021-2022
(Stage - II: Selection of teams to participate in finals)
The objective of LBS is to encourage students to exhibit various innovative theme-based projects on emerging technologies, develop interaction of the students with the Faculty members, enhance team building ability and to provide a competitive environment for students to exhibit their project making skills and motivate the student to come up with innovative ideas for problems based on themes.
