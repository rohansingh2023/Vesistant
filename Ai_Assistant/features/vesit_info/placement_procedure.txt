


Information about the company profile,  job profile, Eligibility criteria, Salary, Branches applicable and any another information given by company are forwarded to all students along with a registration form with the dates of the drive (If specified by Company)
Eligible and interested students are required to register within the specified date and time with additional data if required
The detail database of the interested and Eligible students is sent to the company.
The placement cell along with student coordinators  ensure a smooth conduction of the online / offline process on the given date and time.
This coordination is continued till the select list is declared and offer letters are accepted.


Note:


Students who do not follow the above rules will be blacklisted from the placement procedure. Any other case will be treated as special case and the placement cell will take the necessary action.
All decisions of the placement officer will be final and binding on all.
